About this map

A community-built resource map for Utopia: Origin, covering mines, food sources, creature spawns, chests, and more across the world of Beia. Work in progress — coverage is incomplete.

Using the map
  • Pan and zoom with mouse or touch
  • Search by name, island, or notes in the left sidebar
  • Filter by resource type or island
  • Click any dot to see its details and coordinates
  • Coordinates in the bottom-right show your current map position in game coords
Adding nodes yourself

Hit + Node in the top right to open the node editor. There are two ways to add nodes:

  • Manual — pick type, resource name, and island from the dropdowns, then enter the X and Y shown under your minimap in-game
  • Scan Screenshots — drop in screenshots taken while standing on a resource. Coordinates are read automatically, then you pick type and island and add them all at once

When done, go to the Output tab, copy the JSON, and replace map-nodes.json in the repo — or send it to me to add.

Submitting nodes

Coordinates are read directly from the in-game minimap (No.3Archipelago / X, Y) while standing on a resource. Don't want to deal with GitHub? Just send them to me:

  • Open a GitHub issue with your node data
  • DM on Discord — [gudu0]
